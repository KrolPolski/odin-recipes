Stuffed Pepper

Description

This recipe is a very satisfying sandwich alternative great for low carb diets. It is one of the few low carb meals that doesn't make me feel like I am starving to death.

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Green olives
  • Diced onion
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Split pepper in half with knife and then remove the stem and seeds.
  2. Add feta cheese, olives and diced onion to pepper halves.
  3. Add 1/2 tbsp of olive oil to each pepper.
  4. Cover with turkey breast slices.
  5. Enjoy!
